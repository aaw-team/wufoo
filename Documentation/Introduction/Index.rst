.. _section-what-it-does:

What does it do?
----------------

This extension lets you add your `Wufoo <https://www.wufoo.com/>`_ forms to your
TYPO3 website.

.. _section-about-wufoo:

What is Wufoo?
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Quoting the answer to this question from https://www.wufoo.com/why-wufoo/:

*"Wufoo is the fastest, easiest way to build amazing online forms. Our intuitive
features help you get your form up and running within minutes. No coding
required. But if you're CSS-inclined, never fear! Wufoo forms are completely
customizable and ready to do your bidding."*

For further information go to the Wufoo website: https://www.wufoo.com/
